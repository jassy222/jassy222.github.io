Research

As a researcher, I have published on queer composition practice, significant women composers of experimental and new music, Australian composition and digital music notation. I completed my PhD in Music Composition in 2025 with the title 'Knots that Bind: A Queer Exploration of Censorship and Silence through Experimental Opera.'
